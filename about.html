Author & Host

Greg Barris

Greg Barris is a comedian, writer, and musician known for his sharp wit and transformative creativity. He's the creator of one of the longest-running celebrated cult comedy shows in New York and Los Angeles and the host of the podcast Deep Healing. He has appeared on The Late Late Show and been profiled in The New Yorker and Rolling Stone. Barris lives in Los Angeles where he continues to mix humor, music, pizza, and the metaphysical in unexpected ways. He is interested in exploring experiences that are relational and not purely transactional.

Co-Creator & Publisher

Michelle Newman

Michelle Newman is an award-winning writer, host, and podcast producer. She founded Newman Media, a creative studio dedicated to producing original, genre-blending audio content and print media. Based in Los Angeles, she is also the creator of Tell Me a Ghost Story, an award-winning podcast that merges real supernatural encounters with humor and heart. Her work draws from a deep love of the unexplained and years of experience crafting projects that stick with you long after they end. She has a passion for transforming narratives to reach their full potential.
